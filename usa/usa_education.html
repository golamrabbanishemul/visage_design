USA Education
Study in the USA

If you are thinking of studying in America you are not alone, as the US is now the most popular destination for international students. There are many reasons to choose to study in the United States including:

The quality of education

The US has one of the best education systems with world class programs in virtually every field. Over 30 of the world’s best universities are there, as ranked by Quacquarelli Symonds World University Rankings for 2015/2016. Classes are often small and there may be only 10 to 20 students in a class.

Variety and Choice

The United States has several thousand universities and colleges with institutions specialising in anything you are likely to want to study. With massive campuses that feel like cities, to small, intimate campuses there is certain to be what you are looking for.

Diversity

US universities put a high value on diversity meaning classes will consist of students of all ages, religions, and countries of origin. This creates a learning environment which encourages cross-cultural understanding and networking.

Technology

US institutions take pride in their facilities and seek to offer their students the best technology, whether lab equipment or facilities for research and teaching.

International Student Support

Most colleges and universities will have an international student office dedicated to helping with issues such as visas, financial situations, housing, employment possibilities and health concerns.

Campus Life

When you choose to study in the United States you will broaden your cultural experience, join sports teams, or clubs, meet a wide variety of people and make many new friends. You will also have the chance to explore the United States itself and visit tropical islands, arid deserts, snowy plains; the Grand Canyon, Las Vegas and New York City.

Employment Prospects

International students in the US can work in a job on campus for up to 20 hours a week and all US institutions have a career services department staffed with professionals focused on helping students with their CV, teaching job interview skills, and helping them find job opportunities, both during and after their studies.
